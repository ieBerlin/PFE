OUR VISION

A VISION YOU CAN TRUST

At NESTA, our future is focused on serving and educating fitness & nutrition professionals as both leaders and motivators of personal health, fitness and wellness. With a rich history of pioneering organizational achievements, we remain committed to advancing Personal Fitness Trainers, future-forward.

Our vision connects the skills and commitment needed to address global health and fitness needs with the communities we serve and the industry we represent. NESTA will continue developing fitness professionals that are trained to exceed the expectations for each client served.
